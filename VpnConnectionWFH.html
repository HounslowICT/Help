How to Connect to VPN?
1. Login to your Laptop 2. Click on WIFI icon on your Taskbar at the botton of yor screen 3. Click on your WIFI from the available list 4. Click on Connect and enter your WIFI Password 5. Cisco AnyConnect appears and request your user credentials 6. Enter 6 digits SecurEnvoy passcode from your phone and click Continue. 7 A padlock displays on the AnyConnnect icon on the Taskbar to confirm your connection is complete. Video
Why I Cannot Connect?
Before contacting ICT you can perform a couple of checks to discard common issues such as: make sure device is connected to WIFI if not, try 1. Reset yout WIFI connection or 2.Restart your home router. any of these actions will trigger the Cisco AnyConnect needed to get you connected to VPN.
Installing Securenvoy Authenticator App on my Phone
If you have an Adroid phone you can visit Google Play and type Securenvoy Authenticator in the Search box If you have an Apple Phone visit App Store and type Securenvoy Authenticator in the Search box.
No Active Token Message
Call 3838 to ask ICT to email you an New Token and follow the instructions to enrol you device to the organization.
