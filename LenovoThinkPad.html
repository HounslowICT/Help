Issues With Your Lenovo ThinkPad Laptop

Try this...

Frozen/Switching Device OFF
If you device is irresponsive, you can switch it off holding the Power button down till the light stops flashing, then press and release the power button again. Your device should start normally
No Powering ON - PIN Reset
If your device is unable to Power ON or the battery charger is not charging the device (light indicator beside to charger connection port is not ON). You can try PIN resetting the battery. See this VIDEO for instructions before contacting Help Desk.
