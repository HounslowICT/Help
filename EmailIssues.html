Problem with your Emails?

Try this...

Unable to send/receive emails
Extremely big Inboxes (over 8000 emails) affects Outlook and its capacity to send and receive emails. Delete old emails. See the following steps to DELETE OLD EMAILS for a quick guide. Also clean up our Ibox is using ARCHIVING old emails. If your are not allowed to perform this action, you need to contact ICT to activate this option for you. If any of this actions wont help, Please contact ICT on 020 8583 3838 or log a call via GetITDone Portal.
Inbox Full
Delete old emails as a big number of them could affect Outlook to send and receive emails or making it work slow. See the following steps to DELETE OLD EMAILS for guide. Also, clean up your Inbox using ARCHIVING old emails, bear in mind if your are not allowed to perform this action, you need to contact ICT to activate this option for you. If any of this actions would not help, Please contact ICT on 020 8583 3838 or log a call via GetITDone Portal.
Adding Shared Mailboxes
If your role requires access to Shared Mailboxes, permission for it should be requested by your Line Manager via Sevice Request using SelfService Portal (Hounslow Intranet) once the permission has been granted by ICT you will be able to add it to you Outlook profile. Follow this quick guide to add your SHARED MAILBOX . Still need help? Contact ICT at 020 8583 3838 or log al ticket via GetITDone Portal.
Recalling Emails
If you send and email incorrectly you can recall themessage but keep in mind you can only recall before the recipients have opened the message. Follow this simple steps to RECALL EMAIL
Setting Automatic Response
Use automatic (Out of Office) replies from Outlook for Windows to tell people you won't be responding right away to their email messages. Follow this easy step to set a personal OUT OF OFFICE message
Teams' Link Meeting Disappeared
If Unable to send the Teams' Link to your colleagues in Meetings Invitations perhaps you need to activate Teams Addin to your Outlook. Follow these simple steps to activate your TEAMS LINK and include it on your meetings invite. If still need help please call 020 8583 3838 or log a call using GetITDone Portal
