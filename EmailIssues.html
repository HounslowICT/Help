Problem with your Emails?

Try this...

Unable to send/receive emails
Sometimes extremely big Inboxes (over 8000 emails) tent to affect Outlook and its capacity to send and receive emails. It is a good practice to Delete old emails as a big number of them in your Inbox could affect Outlook affecting the ability to send and receive emails or making it work slow. See the following steps to DELETE OLD EMAILS for a quick guide. Another way to clean up our Ibox is using ARCHIVING old emails, bear in mind if your are not allowed to perform this action, you need to contact ICT to activate this option for you. It is advisable to keep your inbox under 8 000 emails. If any of this actions wont help, Please contact ICT on 020 8583 3838 or log a call via GetITDone Portal.
