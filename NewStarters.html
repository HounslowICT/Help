Important Information...

Setting UP My New Device
Now that you have picked up your new equipment the next step is to complete the set up with your Microsoft account. As there are three types of devices follow this links to useful videos for the type of the device that has been allocated to you. It could be one of these: Lenovo Autopilot device, Microsoft Surface Pro and Lenovo ThinkPad Laptop
Where Can I Find Information
There is lots of useful information in Hounslow Intranet. Open any web browser > type “Hounslow intranet” in the address box> press enter> Navigate to the subject of your interest on the left.
About My Training/Learning Pool
Your training Pool account should be requested by your manager, an account will be created, you will receive and email with your credentials and instructions on how to access it. If you need to enquire about the status of this request call 3838 quoting your request reference number (get it from your manager). .
What is ICT GetITDone Portal?
One way to get IT support is the GetITDone Portal which can be found at Hounslow Intranet. Type "Hounslow Intranet" in the web browser address box and press enter > Navigate to ICT Self Service Portal VIDEO. You can also call 02085833838.
What About the Apps I need for my Work?
If your role implies the use of specific software to be installed on your device, it should be requested by your Line Manager. If you need to inquire about the status of such request, you can call 020 8583 3838 quoting the reference number. Bear in mind that Microsoft Applications are installed as standard.
Am I Getting a Phone?
Smart/Basic Phones or 8x8 Account (to be able to make calls using Teams) are requested by line managers. If you need to inquire about the status of such request, you can call 020 8583 3838 quoting the reference number
